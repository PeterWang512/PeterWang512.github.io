I am a student researcher at BAIR. Recently, I received my BA degree from UC Berkeley where I spent one wonderful year at BAIR, working with Dr. Oliver Wang, Dr. Richard Zhang, Professor Andrew Owens and Professor Alexei (Alyosha) Efros. Prior to joining BAIR, I worked with Dr. Fisher Yu at Berkeley DeepDrive and Professor Michael Crommie on condensed matter physics research.

Research

I started from understanding the structures and properties of materials to those of perception. I am currently interested in computer vision, graphics, and machine learning. I am especially passionate how we can extract low-level and high-level ideas from sensory data, and leverage them for solving interesting tasks such as forgery detection and high-level manipulation.
