I am a first year PhD student researcher at CMU. My research interests are in computer graphics, computational photography, and machine learning. I received BA from UC Berkeley where I spent one wonderful year at BAIR, working with Oliver Wang, Richard Zhang, Andrew Owens and Alexei (Alyosha) Efros. Prior to joining BAIR, I worked with Fisher Yu at Berkeley DeepDrive and Michael Crommie on condensed matter physics research.
